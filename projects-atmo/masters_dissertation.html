Modelling the Sea Surface of Cardigan Bay

ABOUT

A Master's degree, collaborative research project with QinetiQ. The purpose was to obtain a model of the wave's frequency as an input for radar.
Initially worked on implementing two industry-standard models, WAVEWATCH III and Simulating Waves Nearshore (SWAN). They focused on wave propagation and action density, combining series of complex equations to produce results. This approach was untennable, as there were several errors with legacy software, specifically the Fortran 90 compiler.

Wave Models

The initial approach was directed towards existing wavemodels. WAVEWATCH III, produced by the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) was developed.
